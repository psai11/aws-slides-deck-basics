VPC 07
Virtual Private Cloud

Your VPC is a private network in one Region.

  • ·  Logically isolated — nothing reaches in unless you let it.
  • ·  Lives in exactly one Region.
  • ·  Spans one or many AZs (each subnet in one AZ).
  • ·  You pick its IPv4 CIDR — e.g. 10.0.0.0/16.
Your private datacenter network in the cloud.
design rule One VPC ≠ multiple Regions. Plan accordingly.
ok Multiple VPCs in one Region is fine and common.
Reinforce the pattern: VPC = private network, one per Region per design. Multiple VPCs per Region is fine; stretching one across Regions is not.
