Subnet

A subnet is a slice of the VPC, in one AZ.

  • ·  One subnet = one AZ. Always.
  • ·  Resources placed inside get IPs from its CIDR.
  • ·  Split by tier (web vs db) and by AZ for HA.
"One subnet, one AZ" is load-bearing. Many design decisions flow from it. Multi-AZ requires multiple subnets.
