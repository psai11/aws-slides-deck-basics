CIDR notation

Drag the prefix — feel the trade-off.

VPCs and subnets must agree: every subnet's CIDR sits inside the VPC's, and they don't overlap.

The mistake is reading /N as size — it's the opposite. Bigger N, smaller block. Drag the slider to feel it; mention real-world: VPC /16, subnets /24 each.
