Mental model

It nests, like Russian dolls.

One VPC sits in one Region. One subnet sits in one AZ. The rest follows.

remember Region and AZ are not synonyms. A Region contains AZs.
Spine of the talk. Click each layer as you say its name. Don't rush — let them absorb that "Region" and "AZ" are not synonyms.
