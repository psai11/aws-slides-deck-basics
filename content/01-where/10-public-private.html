Public vs private 10
Routing decides

"Public" and "private" describe routing, not address ranges.

key insight Same subnet, same IP range. The route table is what makes it public or private.
Common confusion: people think "public" means a numeric range. It doesn't. The same 10.0.x.x range can be public or private — the difference is what the route table says. Toggle live to drive it home.
