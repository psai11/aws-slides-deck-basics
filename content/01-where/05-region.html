Region 05
Region

A Region is the geographic area you pick.

Region = the city metro area.
choose You pick one in the console — us-east-1, eu-west-1, ap-northeast-1.
tied to Most resources (VPC, EC2, RDS) are regional. Created in — and tied to — that Region.
varies Pricing, service availability, and latency to your users all differ Region by Region.
The hook is "city metro area." Picking a Region is one of the first decisions and it has compliance and latency implications.
