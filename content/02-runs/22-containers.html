Fargate is the how, not the what — pair it with ECS or EKS.
tip Beginners → ECS + Fargate. Teams already on K8s → EKS.
The triangle confuses people. ECS/EKS are orchestrators; Fargate is a capacity model. You don't pick between Fargate and ECS — you pick ECS *with* Fargate.
