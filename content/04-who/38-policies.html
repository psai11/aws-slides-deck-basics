Policies

A policy is the rule book.

Policies answer: which actions on which resources under which conditions?

· AWS-managed  — maintained by AWS
· Customer-managed  — you create, reusable
· Inline  — embedded on one identity
A policy does nothing until you attach it to an identity.
Triangle is the model. Three types: AWS-managed for common stuff, customer-managed for reusable rules, inline for one-offs.
