IAM roles

A role is a hat — you put it on, it expires.

  • ·  No long-term keys on the role itself.
  • ·  Trust policy — who can assume.
  • ·  Permission policy — what the role can do.
used by EC2 instance profile · Lambda execution role · cross-account access · federated humans.
Roles are the right answer to almost every "service X talks to service Y" question. Temporary credentials are the safety mechanism — they expire if leaked.
