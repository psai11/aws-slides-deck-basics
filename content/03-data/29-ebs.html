EBS 29
Elastic Block Store

EBS is a virtual disk. AZ-scoped.

  • ·  Volume in one AZ, attached to one EC2 (multi-attach is the exception).
  • ·  Persistent — survives stop. Resizable. Snapshots are Regional (stored in S3 behind the scenes).
  • ·  Volume types: gp3 default · io2 demanding DBs · st1/sc1 throughput.
Side note — there's also instance store: fast, host-local SSD that vanishes on stop. Save it for a question.
Two takeaways: EBS = one disk, one AZ, one EC2 — and snapshots cross AZs and Regions. Instance store mention is in the margin on purpose; don't dwell.
