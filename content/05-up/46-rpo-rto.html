RPO & RTO

Two numbers, set before you build.

RPO = how stale can data be?
RTO = how long can recovery take?
rule Set these with the business — not in a vacuum.
Not engineering numbers — business decisions you support with engineering. If you don't set them, your architecture sets them by accident. Drag the sliders to give the audience the four DR strategies via play before the spectrum slide.
