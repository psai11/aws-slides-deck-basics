Sync vs async 49
Replication modes

Wait for the copy, or trust it'll catch up.

sync Stronger consistency · higher latency · distance-limited.
async Lower latency · possible data loss on failure · common cross-Region.

Sync goes with HA in-Region; async with DR cross-Region. Almost always.

Grounds the rest of the chapter. Click "send write" to play both sequences. Sync's ACK arrives last; async's ACK arrives before the replica catches up.
