Replication by service 54
Patterns

A scan of replication patterns.

service                pattern                                   mode      scope
─────────────────────  ────────────────────────────────────────  ────────  ─────────────
S3                     SRR / CRR (new objects, optional deletes) async     same/cross-Region
RDS Multi-AZ           synchronous standby in another AZ         sync      in-Region (HA)
RDS read replicas      async replicas, separate endpoint         async     in-/cross-Region
Aurora Global          fast cross-Region replication             async     cross-Region (DR)
DynamoDB global tables multi-Region active-active                async     cross-Region
EBS                    no continuous mirror — snapshot + copy    n/a       backup pattern
EFS replication        cross-Region or in-Region                 async     either
always Check current docs for your engine and version — patterns shift over time.
Don't memorise. Patterns differ per service. Takeaway: most cross-Region is async; managed-DB in-Region tends to sync.
