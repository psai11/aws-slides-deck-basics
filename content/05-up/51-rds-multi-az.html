RDS Multi-AZ

A synchronous standby in another AZ.

  • ·  Primary in AZ-A, synchronous standby in AZ-B.
  • ·  Automatic failover on primary failure (DNS swap, ~60–120s).
  • ·  Standby is not a read endpoint — failover only.
One checkbox at create time, lifetime of dividends.
don't confuse Read replicas are different — async, separate endpoint, can be cross-Region.
Most concrete win in the talk. Checkbox at create time, RDS becomes AZ-resilient. Don't confuse with read replicas — different feature.
