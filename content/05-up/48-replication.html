Replication 48
Backups vs replication

Replication ≠ backups. Both matter.

backups

Point-in-time

Rewind to "yesterday" or "last week." Protect against oops-deletes and corruption.

replication

Running copy

Lag = your RPO floor. S3 SRR/CRR · RDS read replicas · Aurora Global · DynamoDB global tables.

Replication narrows RPO. Backups protect against oops-deletes.
Trap: thinking replication replaces backups. It doesn't — a replicated bad delete is still a bad delete. Both.
